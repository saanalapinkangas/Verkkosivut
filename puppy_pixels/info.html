Information

At Puppy Pixels, we have a deep appreciation for the unique bond between pets and their owners. Our passion is to capture the heartwarming moments, playful antics, and irresistible charm of your beloved pets through the art of photography.

With years of experience in pet photography, our dedicated team understands the nuances of working with animals, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable experience for both pets and their owners. We believe that every pet has a story to tell, and our mission is to narrate those stories through captivating and timeless images.

Whether it's the gleam in your dog's eye during a game of fetch, the tenderness of a cat's purr, or the curiosity of a bunny exploring its surroundings, we aim to freeze these precious moments in time. Our photographers are not just skilled professionals but also pet lovers who connect with your furry family members to bring out their unique personalities.

Your pet is an essential part of your life, and we strive to provide you with photographs that you will cherish forever. From playful portraits to heartwarming candid shots, we specialize in creating a visual legacy of your pets that will warm your heart every time you look at them.

Explore our portfolio and discover the magic that happens when pets and photography come together. Contact us today to schedule a session, and let us capture the joy, love, and companionship that your pets bring into your life.

About me

Hello, I'm Nella, a passionate pet photographer with a love for capturing the beauty and charm of our four-legged friends. My journey into the world of pet photography started with a profound appreciation for the unique bond we share with our canine companions.

As a devoted owner of a beloved Samoyed, I understand the joy and love that dogs bring into our lives. This connection has inspired me to specialize in photographing dogs, aiming to freeze the precious moments, delightful expressions, and playful antics that make our furry friends so endearing.

My photography style is all about authenticity. I believe that dogs should come as they are, without the need for special tricks or extensive training. Each dog has a distinct personality, and my goal is to capture their true essence in every frame. Whether it's a joyful romp in the park, a contemplative moment by the window, or the simple pleasure of a wagging tail, I'm dedicated to preserving these heartfelt memories.
